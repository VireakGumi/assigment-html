Digital Account

More convenient! You can instantly open KB PRASAC Digital Account via KB PRASAC Mobile by using only your phone number and National ID, without visiting any branch.

Open Digital Account and use KB PRASAC Mobile to:

  • Fund transfer within KB PRASAC
  • Fund transfer via Bakong
  • Open Term Deposit Account
  • Scan KHQR for payment
  • Utility & education bill payment
  • Phone Top Up
  • Cash-Code transfer
  • View account balance & transactions

Requirement for Opening Digital Account

  • Cambodian nationality (age 16+)
  • Original valid Cambodian National ID
  • Personal phone number
  • Individual account only
