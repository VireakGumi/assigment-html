ATM

KB PRASAC ATM is always nearby you at anytime and anywhere in Cambodia, and offers you the convenient and safe cash transactions.

With KB PRASAC ATM, you can perform self-banking transactions such as cash withdrawal, fund transfer, Cash-Code, phone top-up, view account balance, and print bank statement.

Currency

You can use KB PRASAC ATM with Khmer Riel and United States Dollar. If a transaction is made in different currency, the system will automatically convert based on KB PRASAC daily exchange rate.

Security Tips of ATM Card Usage

  • Keep your ATM card and PIN secure
  • Do not share your PIN with anyone
  • Cover keypad while entering PIN
  • Report immediately if card is lost or stolen
