Cash-Code

Cash-Code is a type of local money transfer which allows customers to transfer cash to non-account persons by using a security code.

Any customers who have KB PRASAC ATM or KB PRASAC Mobile can transfer cash to their relatives or business partners easily, fast, and securely. Receivers can withdraw cash via KB PRASAC ATMs and all KB PRASAC branches across Cambodia.
